# Trims
- Type R-S
	- Bilstein shocks (softer version)
	* Suede seats
	* Dual oil coolers
	* Front and rear spoilers
	* 17" wheels
	- Larger front and rear brakes (requiring 17" wheels)
		- Front caliper leading piston changed
	- Momo steering wheel
- Touring X
	- 4-speed automatic transmission
	* Luxury suede seats
	* Glass moonroof
	* Fog lights
	* Rear window wiper
	* Bose stereo
	* Automatic climate control
- Type RZ (3)
	- Limited to ? units
	* Only available in Brilliant Black
	* Larger front and rear brakes (requiring 17" wheels)
		* Front caliper leading piston changed
	* Torsen LSD with 4.3 final drive ratio
	* Bilstein shocks (harder version)
	* Recaro front seats
	* 17" BBS wheels
	* No rear seats
	* Driver's knee pad
- Type R Bathurst
	- Limited to 500 units
	- Identical to Type R-S otherwise
- Type R Bathurst X
	- Limited to 777 units
	- Leather seats
	- Gunmetal wheels
	- Identical to Type R-S otherwise
